about

Two disciplines, one method

Everything here comes from the same instinct: take a system apart until you understand why it holds, then rebuild it cleaner. That applies as much to a log parser as it does to a chapter — both get pulled apart, tested against edge cases, and rewritten until the architecture is honest.

Technical work spans Linux internals, open-source contribution, educational frameworks, critical infrastructures, and real estate. Alongside it, a serious literary practice produces creative nonfiction, fiction, and philosophical books under the name Smoke Ascendant.
